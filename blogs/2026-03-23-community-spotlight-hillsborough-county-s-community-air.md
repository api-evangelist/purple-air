The Community Air Monitoring Network (CAMN) is a community-driven initiative designed to collect air quality data to support healthier planning decisions across Hillsborough County, Florida. CAMN brings together community-based organizations, residents, researchers, and public agencies to better understand traffic-related air pollution near interstates on a neighborhood scale.
