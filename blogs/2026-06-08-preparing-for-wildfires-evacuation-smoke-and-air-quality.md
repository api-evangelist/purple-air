Photo by Mathurin NAPOLY on Unsplash Wildfire season is a good time to get ready. Both for the chance you need to leave and for the smoke that can reach far beyond the fire. Preparation falls into two buckets: evacuation (if you're in or near a fire zone) and smoke and air quality (which can affect you even when the fire is miles away).
